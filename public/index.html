Privacy & Security Policy

1. Data Collection: We minimize collection to essential health markers (age, allergies) and prescription history. Images are processed in-memory and deleted instantly.

2. Encryption: All data is stored using E2EE protocols (AES/TLS 1.3). Backups are encrypted at the field level.

3. Retention: Data is kept as long as your account exists. You can trigger a 'Hard Flush' to delete everything anytime.

4. Infrastructure: Hosted on ISO 27001 compliant cloud servers with active firewalling and rate limiting.
